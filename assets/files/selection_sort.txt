             🌹ARABIC🌹
*اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّكَ عَفُوٌ تُحِبُّ الْعَفْوَ فَاعْفُ عَنِّي*

TRANSLITERATION
Allahumma innaka ‘a'fuwwun tuhibbul ‘afwa fa a‘fu ‘anni

TRANSLATION
O Allaah, You are forgiving and You love forgiveness, so forgive me

REFERENCES
[Narrated by Sunan al-Tirmidhi 3513, Nisaee in ‘Amal al-Yawm wa Layla’, Ibn Majah 3850 and Musnad Ahmed, 42/236, No. 25384, Tirmidhi said: this hadeeth is Hasan Saheeh.’ & Al-Albaani in Silsilah Saheehah no. 3337 & Saheeh al-Tirmidhi, 3/170]
